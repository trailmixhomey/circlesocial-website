Getting Started

Circle Social is a private photo and video sharing app for families and close friends. Create invite-only groups, share moments, comment, and stay connected with the people who matter most — with no ads, no algorithms, and no strangers.
To create a group, tap the + button on the Groups screen and give your group a name. To join an existing group, ask a member to send you an invite link — groups are invite-only to keep them private.
Open a group and tap the create-post button. You can select photos and videos from your library, add a caption, and share them with the group. All members of the group will be able to see, comment on, and react to your post.

Account Management

Go to the Profile tab, then tap Edit Profile. You can update your display name, username, bio, and profile photo.
Go to the Profile tab, tap the menu icon in the top right, and select Delete Account. You will be asked to confirm by typing DELETE. This action is permanent — all your posts, comments, and data will be permanently removed. You can also request account deletion by emailing contact@circlesocial.online.
Push notification permissions are managed through your device settings. Go to Settings > Notifications > Circle Social on your iPhone to enable or disable notifications.

Safety & Content

Tap the … menu on any post or comment, then select Report. You can choose a reason (spam, harassment, inappropriate content, or other) and add details. Our team reviews all reports and takes action as needed. You can also report content by emailing contact@circlesocial.online.
Visit the user's profile and tap the menu icon, then select Block. Blocked users will no longer be able to see your content or interact with you. You can view and manage your blocked users from Profile > Menu > Blocked Users.
When you block a user:
  • They can no longer see your posts or profile
  • Their posts and comments are hidden from you
  • They cannot interact with your content
  • You can unblock them at any time from your Blocked Users list
